Fax Parcel Print will guarantee the privacy of all visitors to our site. We will always treat the personal information you provide to us confidentially and in accordance with the Republic Act No. 10173, otherwise known as the Data Privacy Act of 2012. We will only use your data to ensure that your order or request is processed as quickly and properly as possible. For the rest, we will only use this information with your explicit permission, such as for sending our newsletter.

Use of Your Data

Fax Parcel Print uses your personal information to provide the following services:

  • If you place an order or request for quotation, we need your name, e-mail address, delivery/billing address, and possibly your telephone number to execute the order or quotation request and to keep you informed about the progress of this.
  • If you place an order with GleePrinting, we will store your data on a secure server if you desire (an account is created). You can enter a username and password so that your name, address (es), telephone number, e-mail address, delivery, and invoice details do not have to be filled in every new order.
  • To make shopping at GleePrinting as pleasant as possible, we store with your consent your personal details and the details regarding your order and the use of our services, so we can make the website personal.
  • We can use your e-mail address to give you information about the development of our site and about our special offers, products, and promotions. This is only possible after your explicit permission and if you no longer wish to receive this information, you can unsubscribe via our site or via a link in the sent e-mails.
  • GleePrinting will only make your personal data available to third parties involved in the execution of your orders and/or quotation requests and also for that purpose only.
  • Information about the use of our site and the feedback we receive from our visitors helps us to further develop and improve our site.
  • If you decide to write a review, you can choose whether to add your name or other personal information. We are curious about the opinions of our visitors, but reserve the right not to publish contributions that do not comply with our site conditions.
  • If you respond to a campaign or contest, we ask for your name, address, and e-mail address. We use this information to carry out the campaign and to measure the response to our marketing campaigns.

Fax Parcel Print Does Not Sell Your Data

Personal details will never be sold to third parties and will only be made available to third parties if they are involved in the execution of your order or request for quotation. Our employees and third parties engaged by us are obliged to handle the confidentiality of your data in the same way.

The Use of Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of information that your browser stores on your computer when you visit a website. Fax Parcel Print uses these cookies to recognize you on a subsequent visit to our site. These cookies allow us to collect information about the use of our services with the aim of improving and further adapting them to the wishes of our visitors. Our cookies provide information regarding personal identification with the sole purpose of making these improvements and adjustments possible. You can also set your browser so that you do not allow cookies while shopping at Fax Parcel Print.

Got a Question?

If you have any questions about the Privacy Policy of Fax Parcel Print, you can contact us. Our customer service will help you further if you need information about your data stored at Fax Parcel.

Privacy Policy Changes

In case a change of our Privacy Policy should be necessary, you will always find the most recent version on this page.
