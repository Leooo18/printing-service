About Fax Parcel Print

We Love Print

Order your prints easily online

Fax & Parcel, Inc. was established in 1991 to promote goods, courier, telecommunication, and business services to the general public. Among the services are Fax transmission & reception, long-distance telephone (international and domestic), parcel pick-up and delivery & postal services.

About Fax Parcel Print

In 1996, the printing department was shifted from advertising needs into desktop publishing, a while-you-wait design & print service which caters to small business enterprises. Among the services that were offered where design & layout, while-u-wait printing services, volume print office forms (risograph) & lamination services.

By 2001, we have evolved our services by purchasing quality high-volume digital presses while still maintaining the concept of while-u-wait services. Among the print products that can be availed while you wait in shop are: Calling Cards, Invitations, Gift Tags, Sticker Labels, Tickets, Posters, Banners, Flyers, Brochures, Tarpaulin, Photo Mosaic, T-Shirt Transfer, PVC-ID, Risograph, Button Pins, Calendars, Plastic Lamination, Large Format Lamination, Large Format Prints, Digital Photocopy, Finishing Services (Perfect Bind, Plastic Bind, Metal Bind, etc.), Digital Photobook, Manuals, Yearbooks, etc.

Growth and Expansion

In 2006, we changed our corporate name to Fax Parcel Print and we acquired our first state-of-the-art digital presses that can print high volume with a fast turnaround time. We added into our services the Variable Printing, Print on demand services, Book publishing, Photo Album, Personalized Letters, Training Manuals, Proposals, Company Profiles, etc.

We opened our first branch in 2005 in SM Mall of Asia, 2007 in SM North Edsa, 2009 in Ayala Marquee Mall, in September 2011 in Eastwood Cybermall, 2012 in Lucky Chinatown and The District Imus, 2013 in SM Aura Premier, March 2014 in SM Dasmari√±as, October 2016 in SM Cherry in the year 2017 SM Molino, 2018 SM Market Mall BGC, 2018 Gateway Tower Mall and the most recent in 2019 SM Olongapo.

Fax Parcel Print has integrated all of the above services and products into its fold, projecting a one-stop shop to continuing business and public service excellence.
